## Top risks (highest priority)

1. **R1** — ADR supersession. Without it, nothing starts.
2. **R2** — Numerical parity. The whole point of preserving domain vocabulary is to preserve validated maths; if parity drifts, the rewrite is not "clean", it's "regression".
3. **R25** — Resolved. All implementation decisions (D1–D20) confirmed Accepted; no batch will stall on human input.
4. **R12** — Manifest content change from `TechnicalManifestExportNode` redesign. Golden fixture may need regeneration.
5. **R13** — Evidence adapter `store` removal. 42 adapters; one signature change.
